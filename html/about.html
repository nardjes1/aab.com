About Us

AAB is an non profit organization in Algeria, located in Oran, with the aim of spreading the scientific knowledge related to Astronomy. We value the space science and related sciences and we do our best to help increase the interest of the youth and the young generation in such thoughtful sciences. AAB is a perfect place for people with innovative ideas, imagination and curiosity to learn about our universe. Our events for the community and for our members demonstrate our vision, our hard work and our love towards seeking knowledge. AAB welcomes members, volunteers and interested minds in the mysteries of our universe.
